About Me

The Story Behind the Code

I'm Arfa Munam, a Computer Science student who discovered that the intersection of design, development, and AI is where I belong. I build interfaces that feel alive — responsive, polished, and purposeful.

My journey started with frontend development — HTML, CSS, JavaScript — and quickly expanded into AI-assisted workflows. Today I combine both worlds to ship digital products faster and better than ever before.

I'm passionate about Prompt Engineering and exploring how tools like Claude, ChatGPT, and Bolt AI can amplify what's possible for a solo developer. I believe the future belongs to developers who understand AI as a creative partner.
